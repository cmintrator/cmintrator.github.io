Personal Profile

Who is Caitlin? Get a (really good) sense, below.

Caitlin Intrator opens doors, constantly seeks adventure and challenges, and makes things happen. Her background in operations, logistics, and production make her a natural at scaling start ups and small businesses/departments. Caitlin is known for her experience with organizational development and navigating personal and professional transitions; change management is one of her fortes.

People who know Caitlin personally and professionally say things like, “she is… audacious (in her desire to relocate to Tokyo on a mission to resolve a foreign operational problem), a brain on wheels, a partnership guru, an embodiment of the ‘assume good intent’ approach, a person with impeccable standards and values…” Caitlin believes that growth is the most important thing there is and this belief informs her personal and professional strategies. Also, she has concluded, through her experiences, that people have a fundamental need to feel connected. Caitlin helps others overcome challenges by empathizing and partnering with them to develop solutions. She helps her partners and clients to feel heard and understood, to identify opportunities, and provides direct connections to others.
